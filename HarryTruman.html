Fun Facts

Harry Truman was the 33rd president of the United States, serving from 1945 to 1953, succeeding upon the death of Franklin D. Roosevelt after serving as the 34th vice president. He is known for his monumental decision to drop the atomic bomb on Japanese cities Hiroshima and Nagasaki to end World War II. He led America into the Korean War, and helped to found the United Nations.
  • He did not have a vice president
  • His middle name was "S"
  • He was vice president for only 82 days
  • As president, he almost doubled the minimum wage
  • He was in the national guard and served as an artillery commander in World War I
